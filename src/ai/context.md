# Endministrator (Endmin)

## Description

> _"I'm ready."_ — The Endministrator of Endfield Industries

According to the historical records of Talos-II, the Endministrator stands as a key guardian who protected civilization on this planet and saved humanity from multiple catastrophic disasters.

The Endmin's heroic deeds have given rise to many stories, tales, and even rumors. However, the Endmin's identity remains elusive, and only a handful of people have seen the legend in person.

Other factions within the Civilization Band are aware that Endfield holds a very special trump card—but that is all they know.

The Endministrator’s mysterious mastery over **Originium** and the **Protocol Network**, along with the need for periodic long-term hibernation, is one of Endfield’s most important secrets.

---

## About Endmin

The Endministrator of Endfield Industries is recorded in the history of Talos-II as a central guardian figure who preserved civilization and saved humanity from multiple catastrophic disasters.

Despite these achievements:

- Their identity remains unknown
- Only a few have ever encountered them directly
- Their existence is surrounded by myths, stories, and speculation

Other factions recognize Endfield’s possession of a powerful hidden asset, but lack further knowledge.

### Key Traits

- Mastery over **Originium**
- Control of the **Protocol Network**
- Requires **periodic long-term hibernation**

## Lore

### OMV Dijiang

- A spacecraft in geostationary orbit above Talos-II
- Current headquarters of **Endfield Industries**
- Powered by a massive **Protocol-Originium core**

**Key Systems:**

- Tele Protocol (TP)
- Automated Industry Complex (AIC)

---

### Ankhors

- Mysterious constructs with unknown origins
- Randomly scattered across Talos-II
- Responsible for creating **Aggeloi**

**Behavior:**

- Absorb nearby natural materials
- Continuously generate hostile entities

---

### Automated Industry Complex (AIC)

- Core technology of Endfield Industries
- Enables miniaturized and modular industrial production

**Capabilities:**

- Rapid deployment of production lines
- Operable by small professional teams
- Fully automated industrial output

---

### Tianshi Pillars

- Column-shaped **Xiranite devices**
- Developed by the **Hongshan Academy of Sciences (HAS)**

**Purpose:**

- Counter the **Blight Tide**

**Mechanism:**

- Disperse Xiranite particles
- Activate particles to weaken Blight effects

**Significance:**

- One of HAS's major scientific achievements
- Combines Originium tech with **Feranmut studies**

---

### Aggelos (Aggeloi)

- Hostile animated entities composed of natural matter
- Feature a distinct halo structure
- Created by **Ankhors** through catalytic construction

**Role in History:**

- Primary threat during humanity’s expansion on Talos-II
- Central enemy in the **Aggeloi War**

---

### Landbreakers

- Armed raiders operating on the fringes of society
- Engage in robbery and violent criminal activity

**Background:**

- Originally unified under conquest ambitions
- Fragmented after defeat

**Current State:**

- Multiple splinter groups with different names
- "Landbreakers" used as a general term for hostile armed factions opposing civilized order
